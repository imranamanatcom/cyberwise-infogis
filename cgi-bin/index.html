Security Awareness Management System
CyberWise includes a comprehensive security awareness management system that contains basic and advance programs for cyber security awareness for all departments across the corporate. The programs are assigned automatically to employees and can be monitored by the security governance team through interactive dashboards.
Corporate Compliance Management System
CyberWise Corporate Compliance Management ensures that the corporate policies and procedures related to cyber governance reach out to all employees and the entire corporate is compliant to the security initiatives such as Information Security Management Systems (ISO 27001), PCI-DSS, IT Service Management (ISO 20000) & Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery (ISO 22301) etc through automated monitoring.
