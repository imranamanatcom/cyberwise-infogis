CyberWise includes comprehensive modules for Security Awareness, Compliance Management, Security Advisory Management & Reporting. It includes over 100+ modules on security awareness with the workflow for automated assignment of modules to employees with complete quiz, certificate award and escalation workflow with the ability to add your own customize content.


CyberWise also includes Corporate Compliance Management module through which we ensure that all corporate policies and procedures related to cyber governance reach out to all employees and the entire corporate is aligned with the security initiatives such as Information Security Management Systems (ISO 27001), PCI-DSS, IT Service Management (ISO 20000) & Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery (ISO 22301) etc through automated monitoring.


CyberWise facilitates cyber security teams within an organization to release security advisories in SCORM compliant portlets with a time bound follow up and escalation.

Key Benefits
  • Automation & Awareness of Corporate Security Policies
  • Manage Cyber Security Awareness Campaigns
  • Provides Centralized Dashboard for Cyber Readiness
  • Empowers CISO to embed Cyber Security in Organization Culture
