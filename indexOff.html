CyberGuard

CyberGuard is a security assessment platform that provides external vulnerability assessment & penetration testing services for web applications & external interfaces. CyberGuard helps organizations to identify the security risks to their public interfaces from external threats and helps them mitigate it.

CyberGuard conducts periodic vulnerability scanning on your IT assets and share the results on periodic basis under an agreed service level agreement with highlighted risks and recommendations on how to fix them.

  1. Continuous Third Party Assessment of Security Risks
  2. Scanning of IT Assets from External Perspective
  3. SLA covers periodic reporting on identified vulnerabilities & recommendations on fixing critical vulnerabilities.
  4. Lower Costs of Ownership while maintaining increased layer of security
  5. Flexibility of Monthly, Quarterly & Annual SLA

PhishRod

“95% of all attacks on a network are a result of successful spear phishing”

PhishRod is a phishing simulation service that helps an organization to gauge the level of readiness of its employees against phishing. Through PhishRod, a set of emails with contextual information is send to different employees with the intention to trick them to release their confidential information. If tricked, the employees are directed to the learning modules on security awareness or notified accordingly.

PhishRod Service Level Agreement includes

  1. Custom made phishing traps based on your organization needs
  2. Complete Reporting on Statistics for every campaign
  3. Flexibility in designing phishing campaigns, (# of Emails, # of users, Per Month, Per Year)
  4. Integration with LMS for instant learning after trap.
